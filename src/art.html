Unstarry Night.

A copy... of a copy... of one of the world's most recognisable paintings. Done by Vincent Van Gogh around 1889 using a reed pen, titled "Cypresses in Starry Night". The tree is a Cypress tree, which were associated with death in European culture -- they appear in a lot of Van Gogh's paintings. Some think The Starry Night was influenced by photographs through a telescope of spiral galaxies that had been published in popular media of the time.
