Welcome to my website!

I'm an astrophysicist-turned-neuroscientist based in Boston. I'm pursuing a career within cognitive/computational neuroscience,
with broad interests including memory, plasticity, brain augmentation, and the interactions between how we see and hear.
Currently, I'm working as a technical research assistant in Prof Aude Oliva's Computational Perception and Cognition Lab at MIT.
I also enjoy creating music and art.

Within this website you can find out more about the research that I'm interested in, the projects I've been
involved with, and you can check out some of the music and art that I make!
