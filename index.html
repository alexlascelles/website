Welcome to my website!

I'm an astrophysicist-turned-neuroscientist based in Boston. I'm excited by research into how humans see, hear, remember, and interact with the beautifully complex world we find ourselves in.
I enjoy finding creative ways to represent data and communicate it clearly to people of all levels of understanding in an open and honest way.
I believe that by mixing science, technology, and the arts with diversity of thought/background,
we stand the best chance to accelerate our quality of life and tackle the big problems of our time such as global inequality, healthcare, and the climate crisis.
Previously, I worked as a technical research assistant in Dr Aude Oliva's Computational Perception and Cognition Lab at MIT.
I also enjoy creating music and art.

Within this website you can find out more about the research that I'm interested in, the projects I've been
involved with, and you can check out some of the music and art that I make!

Always curious to learn new things, share ideas, and connect with people -- please don't hesistate to get in touch!
